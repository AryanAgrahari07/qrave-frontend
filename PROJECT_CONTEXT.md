1. Product Summary (Elevator Pitch)
Orderzi (QR-Menu-Design) is a QR-powered restaurant operating system that turns any restaurant into a smart, digital-first venue. Guests scan a QR to see a beautiful, multi-language menu, join queues, and interact with the restaurant, while staff run the floor with real-time KDS (Kitchen Display System), waiter terminals, and a built-in POS.
For owners, Orderzi provides a powerful dashboard for menu management, QR code generation, analytics, staff & inventory management, and brand customization, all in a single, easy-to-use platform that works on web and as a mobile app (via Capacitor).
2. Target Audience & Personas
Restaurant Owners / Managers
Small to mid-sized restaurants, cafés, bars, quick-service outlets.
Want to modernize quickly without big hardware or IT investments.
Care about: table turns, average order value, staff efficiency, customer experience.
Front-of-House Staff (Waiters / Hosts)
Need a clear overview of tables, queues, and orders.
Care about: speed of service, accurate orders, smoother communication with kitchen.
Back-of-House Staff (Kitchen)
Need a clean, real-time dashboard of incoming and in-progress orders.
Care about: clear tickets, minimal chaos, fewer mistakes.
Guests / Diners
Tech-comfortable customers who expect QR menus & digital experiences.
Care about: quick access to menu, clarity on items (photos/dietary info), transparency on queue/wait times.
3. Problem & Value Proposition
Problems Today
Manual menus are hard to update (pricing, items, languages) and impossible to personalize.
Paper KOTs and verbal handoffs create mistakes, delays, and miscommunication between waiters and kitchen.
Guests have no visibility into queue status; calling the restaurant or standing in line is frustrating.
Owners lack real-time analytics on what’s selling, when, and where.
Managing staff, inventory, and QR codes is often spread across multiple tools or spreadsheets.
Orderzi’s Core Value
Digitize the full restaurant journey from discovery → seating/queue → ordering → kitchen → billing → analytics.
Optimize operations with real-time visibility for waiters and kitchen staff.
Delight guests with modern QR-based experiences and clear menu information.
Empower owners with live dashboards and simple tools to change menus, prices, and branding instantly.

5. Core Feature Breakdown
5.1 QR Menus & Public Guest Experience
Scan-to-menu:
Each table or entry point has a QR that opens a branded, mobile-friendly menu.
Guests see categories, item names, descriptions, prices, and optional images.
Dietary labels (e.g., veg/non-veg/vegan) and customizations (size, toppings, add-ons).
Multi-language support:
Menu content can be localized (e.g., English/Hindi/Spanish).
Guests can switch languages on the fly.
Public queue:
QR for queue registration:
Guests enter name, phone, party size.
Get an estimated wait time and a live position indicator.
Public status page for guests to check if they’re close to seating.
Restaurant info:
Address, Google Maps link, contact details.
Optional logo and brand colors for a polished look.
5.2 Queue & Floor Management
Queue dashboard:
Staff view of all waiting parties with filters and stats.
Actions:
Call next guest.
Seat guest (assign to a table).
Cancel or update entries with reasons.
Floor map & tables:
Visual representation of table layout by section.
Color-coded table statuses.
Assign waiters to tables.
Manage reservations, blocking tables, and freeing them.
5.3 Orders, POS & Billing
Order handling:
Create new orders (linked to a table or guest).
Add items with variants/modifiers and notes.
Update statuses: pending → preparing → ready → served → closed.
Cancel or split/correct orders (with reasons).
POS interface:
Desktop POS for counter or back-office usage.
Mobile POS for waiters:
Fast item search and category browsing.
Cart view with line-item editing.
Summary with tax, service charge, discount, and total.
Billing & payment:
Integration-ready with payment gateways like Razorpay.
Track payment status and methods per order.
Generate receipts:
Thermal printer formatted.
WhatsApp message templates.
Export transactions for accounting.
5.4 Kitchen Display System (KDS)
Kitchen view:
Tiles or columns for orders grouped by status or time.
Each order shows table/guest, items, quantities, notes.
Operational flow:
Kitchen marks items or orders as in-progress/ready.
Front-of-house views update in real-time.
Objective:
Reduce confusion, missed items, and miscommunication between waiters and kitchen.
5.5 Waiter Terminal
Single control center:
Floor map with table statuses and assignment.
Current queue with actions.
List of active orders per waiter or per section.
Embedded POS (open POS for a table, add items, close bill).
Benefits:
Waiters work from one interface.
Faster table turnover and fewer manual trips to the kitchen or POS counter.
Better visibility of their section and responsibilities.
5.6 Menu Management & AI Extraction
Menu builder:
CRUD on categories and items.
Configure addons, sizes, combos via variants and modifier groups.
Mark items in/out of stock quickly.
Reorder categories and items for better UX.
AI menu extraction:
Upload existing printed or PDF menus.
System uses OCR/AI to extract structured data (categories/items/prices).
Owner reviews and tweaks the extracted menu.
One-click import into live menu data.
Outcome:
Dramatically reduces onboarding friction.
Simplifies large or frequently changing menus.
5.7 Staff, Inventory & Branding
Staff management:
Create/edit staff with roles (ADMIN/WAITER/KITCHEN).
Reset passcodes and manage activation.
Limit access to appropriate surfaces (KDS vs waiter vs owner dashboard).
Inventory:
Simple materials list with units and current stock.
Reorder thresholds and status.
Foundation for future deep integrations with consumption tracking.
Branding & QR:
Manage restaurant logos (predefined or custom).
Generate QR codes:
For menu, queue, or combined flows.
For each table (table-specific QR).
Track scan activity for QR codes over time.
5.8 Analytics & Reporting
Dashboard overview:
Today’s revenue, orders, active tables, queue stats.
Recent orders and scan activity.
Analytics view:
Revenue over time (daily/weekly/monthly).
Top dishes & categories.
Peak hours and traffic patterns.
Scan analytics: where/when guests are scanning QR codes.
Transactions view:
Detailed transaction list per order with date, method, amount.
CSV export for finance/accounting.
